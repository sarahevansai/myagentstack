## What it does

Researches a thesis, validates outlets against trust tiers, filters out vendor/sales/syndicated junk, and returns a research brief that the writing agents downstream can rely on.

## Why thesis-first

Most AI research agents return whatever's in the top search results. This one starts from a thesis and works backward — only sources that actually support the thesis make it through.
