## What it does

Replaces a LinkedIn scheduler with a full-funnel agent: monitors signals, drafts content, engages on posts, and runs DMs.

## What you get

- 24/7 comment and DM engagement
- Voice-aware content drafting
- Industry signal monitoring tied to content prompts
- Closer-to-human cadence than typical scheduling tools

## Why it matters

LinkedIn rewards consistency and conversation, not posting volume. Meet Lea is an attempt to staff that conversation layer with an agent — which is the part most teams skip.
