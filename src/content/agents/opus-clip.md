## What it does

Reads long videos, identifies the strongest moments, generates short clips with captions, and ranks each clip by predicted virality.

## What you get

- Long-form to short-form video clipping
- AI-powered virality scoring per clip
- Auto-captioning and aspect-ratio formatting
- Built around dialogue-heavy content (podcasts, interviews, webinars)

## Why it matters

Speech-heavy creators usually have months of long-form sitting unused. Opus turns that backlog into shippable short-form, and the virality score takes the guesswork out of which clip to push.
