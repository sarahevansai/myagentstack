## What it does

Sets up a one-screen command center inside Todoist, Monday.com, or ClickUp where every operational link lives — docs, dashboards, logins, tools, projects.

## Why it works

The default state for marketing/comms/ops teams is "where was that link?" This template eliminates the search.
