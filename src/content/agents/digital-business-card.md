## What it does

Generates the full digital business card stack: a QR code for your phone's lock screen wallpaper, a branded contact landing page, and a matching email signature template — all on-brand.

## When to use

Conferences, events, podcast guests, sales demos. Anywhere you used to hand someone a paper card.
