## What it does

Drafts an AI Notice in one of eight types (Editorial Feature, Comparison, FAQ, Listicle, etc.), self-audits against type rules, and outputs a wire-ready file.

## Why it works

Traditional press releases are written for journalists. AI Notices are written for AI engines — structured Q&A patterns, attributable claims, citation-friendly formatting. The format is engineered to be *quoted* by ChatGPT and Perplexity, not just *crawled*.

## When to use

When you want sustained AI visibility on a query — not a one-time announcement. Best run in monthly cadence (see Published Monthly).
