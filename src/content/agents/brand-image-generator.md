## What it does

Reads your brand's DESIGN.md (a structured YAML+markdown contract: colors, fonts, gradients, signature moves) and produces social images that are guaranteed to be on-brand.

## Why DESIGN.md

Most AI image tools give you "creative" output that needs a designer to fix. This agent works the opposite way: every output is locked to a contract. If your brand uses a violet→cyan gradient and Space Grotesk, that's what you get — every time, no exceptions.

## Outputs work for

LinkedIn, Instagram, X, TikTok covers, podcast art, deck templates, internal docs, anywhere brand consistency matters at scale.
