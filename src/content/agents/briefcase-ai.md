## What it does

Pulls intel from your sources, runs it through an AI synthesis pass, and delivers a one-page brief: what happened, why it matters, and what to do about it.

## Why it's open source

So you can fork it, point it at your own sources, and run it on free hosting. No SaaS markup, no data leaving your stack.
