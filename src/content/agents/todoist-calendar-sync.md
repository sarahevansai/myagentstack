## What it does

Two-way real-time sync between Todoist tasks and Google Calendar — task gets a calendar block, calendar block updates the task.

## Why it matters for marketing teams

Everyone says "time block your week." Almost nobody does it because the manual overhead kills the habit. This automates the habit.
