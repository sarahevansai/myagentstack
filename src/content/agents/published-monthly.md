## What it does

Orchestrates the full PM cycle, end to end: planning call, anchor article, SME quote round, AI Notices, compliance QA, GNW submission, and post-distribution reporting.

## Why monthly cadence

AI engines reward sustained presence on a query, not single drops. Published Monthly is engineered for the *cumulative* citation pattern — not one big launch.

## Two flavors

**Standard PM (Answer Share)** — proactive AI visibility for brands that want to own a category.
**Reputation PM** — AI reputation repair for brands with negative AI surface area.
