## What it does

Produces the foundational document that downstream content agents reference — the rules of engagement for everything written for a client.

## Why every client needs one

Without a documented compliance contract, every writer (human or AI) makes their own judgment calls about claims, language, tone, and disclosures. This agent eliminates that variance at the source.
