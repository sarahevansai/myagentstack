## What it does

Recommends journalists to add or remove from your media list based on the actual content of your pitch — and shows you why.

## What you get

- Pitch-aware journalist recommendations from Muck Rack's verified database
- Advisory-only behavior (never sends outreach autonomously)
- Pitch placement markers tied back to coverage in Muck Rack dashboards
- Coverage-alignment widgets for measuring outreach effectiveness

## Why it matters

Most "AI media list" tools generate plausible-looking lists from a static database. Media List Agent is built around the actual pitch text, which is why beta users saw double-digit lift in open and click rates over generic mass pitching.
