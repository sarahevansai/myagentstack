## What it does

Audits any written piece across four dimensions: AI markers (telltale phrases, sentence patterns), AP style, brand voice deviation, and compliance. Returns a fix-list, not a vague rating.

## Why a separate agent

Writing and reviewing are different cognitive tasks. AI tools that try to do both produce drafts that pass their own audits. A separate QA agent — different prompt, different criteria — catches what the writer missed.
