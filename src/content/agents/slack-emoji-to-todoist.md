## What it does

You react to a Slack message with a specific emoji. The Slack-to-Todoist worker sees the reaction and creates a Todoist task with the message content, link back, and any context.

## Why it works

The friction-killer for "I'll come back to this later" Slack messages — which everyone has, and almost nobody actually comes back to.
