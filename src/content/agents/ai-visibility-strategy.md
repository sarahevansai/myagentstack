## What it does

Phase 1 is a workshopped strategy — inline thinking partner that maps your brand to AI search demand. Phase 2 outputs the calendar: headlines, formats, and target prompts for the next 90 days.

## Why GEO is its own discipline

Traditional SEO optimizes for ranking. GEO optimizes for *being cited*. The content patterns, citation structures, and distribution playbooks are different. This agent operates inside the GEO playbook, not the SEO one.
