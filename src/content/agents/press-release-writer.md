## What it does

Drafts a proper press release: dateline, lead, body paragraphs, embedded quotes, boilerplate, contact block. Wire-ready and journalist-readable.

## When to use vs. AI Notice

Press releases are best for hard news — funding rounds, launches, hires. AI Notices are better for thought leadership and visibility plays. This agent will tell you which one your situation needs.
