## What it does

Takes a finished release and produces every field GNW needs in a single table — subjects, keywords, summary, related links, headline variants — optimized for pickup and indexing, validated against publisher rules.

## Why it matters

GNW pickups depend more on the metadata than the content. Most teams paste in defaults and lose 50%+ of their potential pickup. This agent treats the metadata as the SEO surface it actually is.
