## What it does

Writes a single canonical article on the question your audience is actually searching — built using the **Search Intent First** and **Dual Optimization** principles (humans and AI engines).

## When to use

When you need one definitive piece on a topic — the hub everything else links to. Not for short blog posts, social posts, or announcements.

## How it differs

Most AI long-form writers produce content optimized for word count. This agent optimizes for citation: clear answers, structured Q&A patterns, schema-ready headers, and the kind of source-able statements that AI engines pick up and surface.
